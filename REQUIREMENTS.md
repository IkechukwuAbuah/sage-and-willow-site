## Page Sections

### 1. Hero
- Headline: "Plan your legacy across borders"
- Subhead: Cultural competence + dual-jurisdiction expertise
- CTA: Join waitlist button
- Optional: Hero image (sage plant + willow tree?)

### 2. Problem Statement
- Stats from research:
  - 70% of Nigerians die without wills
  - 50-58% of Canadians die intestate
  - Family disputes = 30% of Nigerian civil cases
- Emotional hook: "Your hard work shouldn't disappear"

### 3. Solution / Services
- 8 services in card/grid format:
  1. Digital legacy organization
  2. Asset inventory (dual-jurisdiction)
  3. Will drafting
  4. Power of attorney
  5. Dual-jurisdiction planning
  6. Cultural burial rites
  7. Prepaid funeral planning
  8. Probate guidance

### 4. Who It's For
- Two segments:
  - "Planning ahead" (30s) - New homeowners, new parents
  - "It's time" (60s-70s) - Health events, peer mortality

### 5. Why Us (Differentiators)
- Cultural competence: We understand Nigerian values
- Dual-jurisdiction: Canada + Nigeria expertise
- Integrated: Digital, legal, funeral in one relationship
- Approachable: "Like opening a bank account"

### 6. Survey CTA
- "Help us build the right service"
- "Take our 3-minute survey"
- Incentive: Early access + free planning guide

### 7. Waitlist / Footer
- Email capture (required)
- Phone (optional)
- Privacy note
- Social links (when ready)

## Design Notes

### Tone
- Warm, not clinical
- Security-focused, not death-focused
- Professional but approachable
- Culturally resonant (subtle Nigerian touches)

### Colors (suggestions)
- Sage green (#9CAF88) - Wisdom, calm
- Willow brown (#8B7355) - Earth, roots
- Cream/off-white background
- Dark text for readability

### Typography
- Clean, readable sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans)
- Generous line height
- Clear hierarchy
